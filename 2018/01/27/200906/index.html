         人生设计卡
 姓名　　　　　　  ：　　匿名用户　　　　　　　　　　
 最崇拜的人　　  　：　　爱因斯坦　　　　　　　　　
 人生格言　　　　  ：我自己，我的灵魂，我的理想　　
 而十年后的我　　  ：实现理想，做一个无愧于“生”的人
 今天我该这样度过　 ：　无愧于自己　　　　　　

2017/9/18

Summary, I feel a lot from the scratch I made during high school, I am looking for the reason which made me who I am today. I can find the records back to 8 years ago, with ambiguous words I describe the state of me at that time.

I can see the change during these years, at first, it can be said as scrawl, then it become tidy and consistent.

At high school, I had ambition which I also have no idea how to achieve, I didn’t know that. It now seems clear, great goal takes great efforts, which I have no clear way toward to. I was only driven by imagination, and I was sleepless about my dream, I was deeply immersed in imagination, I became so sensible about everything which I can have enormous different views. Taking a step back, I can only say my life was burning with passion. At that time, I also care little about my personal life since it means little for living my life to full. I pay a lot for personal feelings nowadays.

There is clear dividing line during my life stage. In my college life, me around 21 years old, I dive into computation, logic, It is true work for my daytime. After a couple of years, I became different, I become capable of accepting failure and learned from it.

And Now I am still may in a mess, because at different stage in life, what troubles me varies, I can see the change on me. I feel happy for myself, it’s not easy to come this far.
